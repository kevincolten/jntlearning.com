We Are JNT

Jessica Rogers

Jessica merges her creative expertise with more than two decades of work experience as a public school teacher. Having earned two Master's Degrees, she continues to provide Professional Development through the use of improvisation in the classroom to educators nationwide.  In addition to facilitating corporate trainings, Jessica develops and participates in corporate role-play experiences.  A performer, instructor, and director, Jessica currently teaches at iO and The Second City.  She is also a Company Member of Face Off Unlimited.

Nnamdi Ngwe

Nnamdi Ngwe is a performer, teacher and writer. He works with corporations globally as a corporate trainer/facilitator and specializes in applying the fundamentals of improv in the workplace. These tools are shared in his fun, upbeat workshops and lead to better communication and a much more productive workplace. Nnamdi has won awards for his teaching and continues to perform with his award winning sketch/improv group 3Peat. He can also be seen working with Comedy Central, ABC, Bleacher report, The Ringer, and much more.

Todd Edwards

Todd combines his creative knowledge in teaching and performing improvisation with over 20 years of work experience as a Lead Learning and Development consultant and facilitator.  Additionally, he has developed branded comedy shorts for Serial 1 e-bikes and GlobalGolf.com.  He currently teaches Improv Technique at Cypress College in Cypress, CA. while  working on his Master's in Organizational Leadership.
